Your Move

Common questions & privacy policy · Last updated: 29 June 2026

How does it read my notifications?

With your permission, Your Move uses Android's notification access to read incoming notifications on your phone and pick out the ones that are a real person waiting on a reply. All of this happens on the device. The contents of your notifications are never sent anywhere.

Is it a subscription?

No. Your Move is free, with no subscription, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

Which apps does it work with?

It works with messaging apps that use Android's standard message notifications, including WhatsApp, Signal, Google Messages / SMS, Telegram, and most others. It automatically ignores group chats, news, and promotional notifications, so you only see the people actually waiting on you. (We are still expanding and testing across more apps.) Sending a reply works with apps that offer an inline reply, which most messaging apps do.

What about my privacy?

Everything stays on your phone. The app holds no internet permission at all, so it is technically incapable of sending your data anywhere. What it stores, a sender's name and a short message snippet, is kept encrypted on the device, deleted automatically after 30 days, and you can erase everything any time with "Clear all data" in Settings. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

Privacy Policy

Your Move is an Android app that helps you see which people are waiting on a reply from you. It is built to keep your data on your phone. This policy explains exactly what that means.

The short version

Your Move does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. Nothing leaves your device. The app holds no internet permission, so it is technically incapable of sending your information anywhere.

Notification access

Your Move uses Android's notification access (NotificationListenerService) to read your incoming notifications on your device. This is used for one purpose: to identify the people who are waiting on a reply and to show them to you in the app. The contents of your notifications are processed entirely on your phone and are never sent to us or to any third party.

What we store, and where

To show your list, the app stores a small amount of information locally on your device: the sender's name and a short snippet of the message. This is kept in an encrypted database on your phone. It is never uploaded. Cleared items and anything older than 30 days are deleted automatically, and you can erase everything at any time with "Clear all data" in Settings.

On-device AI (reply drafts)

When the app drafts a reply, it does so using an on-device language model. Your messages are not sent to a server for this. On supported devices the draft is produced by the operating system's on-device AI service, which runs separately from this app; Your Move itself still sends nothing over the network.

What we do NOT do

  • No accounts, no sign-in.
  • No analytics or tracking.
  • No advertising.
  • No third-party data sharing.
  • No internet permission.

Children

Your Move is not directed at children under 13.
